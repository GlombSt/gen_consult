Structured intent specification + insights-driven refinement for better AI task execution.

## VALUE PROPOSITIONS
* Higher-quality results through comprehensive, low ambiguity, semantical rich specification of work. Drastically reduces hallucination/variance in AI usage
* AI-generated insights that surface missing details and ambiguity
* Lower iteration cost by improving inputs, not patching outputs. Stops the "tweak-test-repeat" loop.
* Reusable, evolving intent "recipes" for recurring tasks
* Industry and Domain specific reference
* Learning loop that captures and reuses improvements over time
* "Quality Gate." Scores intent clarity before execution. Rejects vague inputs frontier models would blindly accept
* "Advisor." Suggests the best way to execute (e.g., "Don't prompt; use an Agent"). Surfaces "unknown unknowns"
* Lifting quality of existing prompts

## COST STRUCTURE

* Phase 1 (Variable Risk): High API costs (LLM Inference) as you pay for every "Grade" and "Interview"
* Phase 2 (Stabilized): Costs flatten as heavy users switch to BYOK (paying their own API bills)

## REVENUE STREAMS
* Freemium: limits on intents/executions/insights
* Pro subscription: higher usage + advanced insights
* Team plans: shared intents, collaboration, versioning
* Enterprise: SSO, compliance, audit logs
* Template marketplace revenue (future)
* The "Token Pass" (Launch): Tiered Subscription (e.g., Free: 5 checks/day, Pro: Unlimited). Covers API costs + margin
* The "Power License" (Future): BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) for heavy users. Lower monthly fee, user pays compute
