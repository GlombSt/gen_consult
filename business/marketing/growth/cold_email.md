## Cold E-Mail: Strategie und Prognose

### Ausgangslage

| Metrik | Wert |
|--------|------|
| Zielmarkt | USA, UK, Australien (DSGVO-konform) |
| Zeitbudget | 2h/Woche |
| Setup-Aufwand (einmalig) | 4–6h |

### Infrastruktur

| Komponente | Details | Kosten/Monat |
|------------|---------|--------------|
| Outreach-Domains | 3 Domains (z.B. mail.sharpintent.com, sharpintent.com) | ~€3 |
| Mailboxen | 6 Stück (2 pro Domain) via Google Workspace | ~€36 |
| Cold E-Mail Tool | Instantly.ai | €37 |
| Listenbau | Apollo.io (Free Tier oder €49) | €0–49 |
| **Total** | | **€75–125** |

### Versand-Kapazität

| Metrik | Wert |
|--------|------|
| Mailboxen | 6 |
| E-Mails pro Mailbox/Tag | 50 (nach Warm-up) |
| E-Mails pro Tag | 300 |
| E-Mails pro Monat | ~8.000 |
| E-Mails pro Jahr | ~96.000 |

### Warm-up-Phase (Woche 1–4)

| Woche | E-Mails/Tag/Mailbox | Total/Tag |
|-------|---------------------|-----------|
| 1–2 | 10–20 | 60–120 |
| 3–4 | 30–40 | 180–240 |
| Ab Woche 5 | 50 | 300 |

### Listenbau

**Zielgruppe:**

| Filter | Einstellung |
|--------|-------------|
| Job Title | Head of Marketing, Legal Counsel, Product Manager, Consultant |
| Seniority | Manager, Director, VP |
| Company Size | 50–500 Mitarbeiter |
| Industry | SaaS, Consulting, Legal Services, Marketing Agencies |
| Location | USA, UK, Australia |

**Tools:**

| Tool | Funktion | Kosten |
|------|----------|--------|
| Apollo.io | Lead-Datenbank, Export | €0–49/Monat |
| Hunter.io | E-Mail-Finder, Verifizierung | €49/Monat |
| LinkedIn Sales Navigator | Gezielte Suche (optional) | €80/Monat |

### E-Mail-Sequenz

| Schritt | Timing | Inhalt |
|---------|--------|--------|
| E-Mail 1 | Tag 1 | Problem ansprechen, Neugier wecken |
| E-Mail 2 | Tag 3 | Social Proof oder Use Case |
| E-Mail 3 | Tag 7 | Value geben (Ressource, Insight) |
| E-Mail 4 | Tag 14 | Soft CTA (Demo, Free Trial) |
| E-Mail 5 | Tag 21 | Break-up E-Mail |

### Conversion-Funnel

| Stufe | Rate | Von 10.000 E-Mails |
|-------|------|-------------------|
| Zugestellt | 95% | 9.500 |
| Geöffnet | 30% | 2.850 |
| Geklickt | 3% | 285 |
| Free Signup | 15% | 43 |
| Paid Conversion | 7% | 3 |

**→ ~3 zahlende Kunden pro 10.000 E-Mails**
