# Erkenntnisse

## Brand- und Kontextbeschreibung für Logo-Prompt (SharpIntent)

### Brand Name
**SharpIntent**

### Business Type
B2B SaaS (web-based software)

### Core Purpose
SharpIntent helps professional users and teams formulate, clarify, and assess task definitions before they are executed by large language models. The product focuses on structure, completeness, risk awareness, and consistency in AI task specification, and visualizes progress through lightweight gamification elements such as scores and indicators that reflect the quality and completeness of the defined intent.

### Problem Addressed
Unclear or incomplete task definitions lead to inconsistent, error-prone AI outputs, increased iteration cycles, and hidden risks. SharpIntent reduces ambiguity and improves reliability before execution.

### Target Audience
- Professional knowledge workers
- Consultants, product managers, legal, HR, marketing roles
- Team leads and decision-makers responsible for AI usage
- B2B, freelancers to SMEs and enterprise teams

### Brand Positioning
- Professional, precise, methodical
- Calm, rational, trustworthy
- Tooling for serious work, not experimentation or play
- Emphasis on clarity, intent, structure, and foresight

### Brand Personality Keywords
Structured · Analytical · Neutral · Reliable · Minimal · Disciplined · Thoughtful · Transparent · Progress-oriented

### Emotional Tone
Low-noise, confidence through rigor, no hype, no playfulness

### Design Implications
- Avoid playful, childish, or decorative styles
- Avoid mascots, human figures, or abstract chaos
- Favor clarity, balance, and restraint
- Subtle references to progress, scoring, or advancement are acceptable
- Suitable for professional software, documentation, and decision-support tools

### Visual Identity Inspiration (Paraphrased)
- Emphasis on clarity, openness, and reduction, with deliberately restrained, two-dimensional forms and no decorative excess.
- Strong focus on legibility and precision in typography, prioritizing functional clarity and consistency across digital contexts.
- A disciplined color system with limited, confident tones applied consistently to avoid visual noise.
- Overall impression of technological maturity, trustworthiness, and forward-looking competence, balancing rationality with subtle dynamism.

### Industry Context
AI tooling, prompt engineering support, AI governance, productivity software, professional SaaS
